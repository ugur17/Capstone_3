## Business Context and Decision

 - According to the instructions file, purpose is for EmporiUm to analyze sales performance over the last four years.  
 - Result will include Power BI report that highlights key insights, including trends over time and insights into recent sales highlights, to be shared with the regional sales team.
 - Also identify top-selling books and their

 - A line chart showing sales trends over the full period covered in the data 
 - A column or bar chart showing sales performance by category

 - A list of top-selling books, including author names, for your region (general audience, 
excluding textbooks)
